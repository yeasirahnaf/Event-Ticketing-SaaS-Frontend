# Attendee (User): Design & Content Plan

## 1. Design Plan
- **Theme:** "Event Experience" - Immersive, visually appealing, and trustworthy.
- **Visuals:** High-quality imagery (Hero images for events), clear pricing in BDT, and branded tenant colors.
- **Layout:** Clean storefront grid for browse, single-page scroll for event details, and breadcrumb navigation.
- **Aesthetics:** Premium typography, smooth transitions between pages, and secure-looking checkout elements.

## 2. Page List
- **Tenant Home (`/`):** Featured events, organizer bio, and newsletter signup.
- **Event Detail (`/e/[slug]`):** Description, venue map, price list, and FAQs.
- **Checkout (`/checkout`):** Seat selection (if applicable), discount entry, and payment selection (bKash/Nagad/Cards).
- **My Tickets (`/my-tickets`):** Dashboard for personal ticket passes and order history.
- **Ticket View (`/tickets/[id]`):** Digital QR pass with date, time, and venue details.

## 3. Content Plan
- **Event Grid:** Cards with hero images, date badges, prices starting from à§³X, and booking status.
- **Checkout Components:** Order summary breakdown (Subtotal, Discount, Fees).
- **QR Pass:** Large, easy-to-scan QR code with "Add to Wallet" (future) and "Download PDF" options.
- **Profile Info:** Basic account settings and email preferences.
